# Code of Conduct

As contributors and maintainers of ReactPyx, we pledge to make participation
in our project and community a harassment-free experience for everyone,
regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics,
gender identity and expression, level of experience, education,
_socio-economic_ status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or
sexual identity and orientation.

- Be respectful and inclusive.
- Focus on constructive feedback and technical issues.
- Do not use sexualized language or imagery.
- Do not harass, troll, or insult other participants.

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please open an issue or
contact the maintainers privately via GitHub.

This Code of Conduct is inspired by the [Contributor Covenant][cc].

[cc]: https://www.contributor-covenant.org/
